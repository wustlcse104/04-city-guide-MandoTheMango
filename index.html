Classic Chicago Food:

Deep-Dish Chicago Pizza:

Chicago is the land of food, and Chicago's gooey, cheesy deep-dish pizza will make you forget all about thin crust. Deep-dish pizza is baked in a round pan stuffed with cheese and topped with a ridiculously thick layer of tomato sauce. After it comes out of the oven, you are given a nice gooey, hot, buttery pizza.

Lou Malnati's Pizzeria is a family owned pizzeria. It has been named "Chicago's Best Deep Dish since 1971" Lou Malnati's uses sweet and tangy tomatoes, fresh mozzorella cheese in thier pizzas.

Giordano's is known around Chicago as the best stuffed Pizza. Their pizza has a layer that holds melted cheese, so when you slice into the pizza, cheese oozes out. Giorano's pizza are also 40% bigger than other deep dish pizzas!

Chicago-Style Hot Dogs:

Chicago-stye Hot Dogs are the real deal. An all-beef hot dog on a steamed bun, topped with your favorite toppings, and Protillo's is one of the best places to get fast, cheap, and good hot dogs in Chicago. On top of that, try Protillo's Chocolate Cake Milk Shake!

Have you been hangry (angry and hungry) late at night? Make your way to The Wiener's Circle During the night time, you can enjoy a delicious MaxWell Street Polish hot dog while relieving your stress with the employees as you verbally abuse each other!
